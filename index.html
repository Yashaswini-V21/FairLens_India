Why we built this

AI decisions are fast. Bias harm is faster.

01

The core gap

Most teams ship models with accuracy checks but no fairness audit layer. Sensitive variables can quietly influence high-stakes outcomes with no clear visibility for product or compliance teams.

02

What FairLens changes

FairLens adds a practical governance workflow to existing ML pipelines: detect bias, explain root causes, apply correction, and export evidence-ready reports in one guided interface.

03

Why now

India-scale lending, hiring, education, and public-service systems increasingly rely on algorithmic decisions. Teams need fairness controls before models reach production users.

30-second intuition

How fairness audits work in FairLens.

1

Upload your model

Drag in a trained model (.pkl) and your test dataset (.csv).

2

Mark sensitive features

Select which columns represent protected attributes (gender, caste, religion, etc.).

3

Run bias detection

FairLens measures how much the model treats groups unfairly—demographic parity, equalized odds, and opportunity gaps.

4

Understand the findings

Get top bias risks explaining which features drive unfair decisions, with confidence scores and impact estimates.

5

Correct with Fairlearn

Apply fairness-constrained retraining and see before/after impact on accuracy and parity.

6

Export audit trail

Download a PDF or JSON report with findings, corrections, and governance sign-off for compliance teams.
